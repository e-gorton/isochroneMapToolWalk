Drawing tips

Proposed walk, cycle, shared-use and bridge links are modelling assumptions for the local walking/cycling graph. Draw them so they cross or touch the road/path you want to connect to, then finish the drawing to trigger a refreshed walking or cycling catchment.

Barriers restrict crossed walking/cycling graph edges. Exclusion areas only mask the displayed isochrone fill and do not change the routed network.
